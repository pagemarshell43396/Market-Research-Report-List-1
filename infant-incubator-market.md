<p><strong>The Infant Incubator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transport Infant Incubator</li><li>Normal Infant Incubator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infant incubator market is primarily categorized into two types: transport infant incubators and normal infant incubators. Transport infant incubators are designed for the safe and efficient transport of premature or critically ill infants. They maintain controlled temperatures and provide essential monitoring during transit. In contrast, normal infant incubators are stationary units used in neonatal intensive care units (NICUs) to create a stable environment for vulnerable infants, promoting growth and recovery with features for temperature regulation, humidity control, and monitoring capabilities.</p></p>
