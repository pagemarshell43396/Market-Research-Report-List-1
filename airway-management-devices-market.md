<p><p>The airway management devices market includes various types such as endotracheal tubes, laryngeal mask airways, and others. Endotracheal tubes are utilized for securing the airway during anesthesia or critical care by placing a tube into the trachea. Laryngeal mask airways provide a less invasive alternative, creating a seal over the laryngeal inlet to maintain airway patency while allowing for ventilation. Other devices can include oropharyngeal airways, nasopharyngeal airways, and bag-valve masks, catering to diverse clinical needs.</p></p>
