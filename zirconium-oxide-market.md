<p><ul><li>Electro Fused Zirconium Oxide</li><li>Chemical Zirconium Oxide</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The zirconium oxide market is primarily categorized into two types: electro fused zirconium oxide and chemical zirconium oxide. Electro fused zirconium oxide is produced through the fusion of zirconium sand in electric arc furnaces, resulting in a durable material used in high-performance applications, including ceramics and refractories. In contrast, chemical zirconium oxide is manufactured through chemical processes and is commonly used in applications such as advanced materials, catalysts, and pigments, due to its specific properties and purity levels.</p></p>
